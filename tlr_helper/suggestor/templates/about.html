About TLR Helper

TLR Helper is a tool designed by TEDD Ghana and NileEdge Innovations to support teachers in developing curriculum-aligned Teaching-Learning Resources (TLRs) for the Ghana Standards-Based Curriculum (SBC).

You can search for TLRs based on class level, subject, strand, competency, or learning theme. Each TLR includes guidance on materials, steps, tips, and classroom context â€” making it easier to bring creative, hands-on learning to life.

We believe in empowering teachers to adapt to their classroom needs, learning styles, and special needs, while still delivering outcomes aligned with national standards.

Questions or feedback? Contact the TEDD Ghana team during your next workshop, or through your school coordinator.
